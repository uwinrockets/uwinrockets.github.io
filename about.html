Competition

An on-campus organization dedicated to research development and building of model rockets, in preparation of the 2018 Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition (IREC) Competition in New Mexico.

Focus

Alongside the aerodynamics and materials engineering disciplines associated with rocketry engineering, the division also had a focus of avionics, developing in-house solution for rocket simulation for aerodynamic diagnostics purposes.
